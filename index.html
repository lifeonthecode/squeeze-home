THINK ABOUT A TIME IN YOUR LIFE

When you felt totally misunderstood. When you’d think how can I feel so alone or unnoticed or unappreciated or frustrated or undervalued? Maybe it wasn’t even long ago, maybe it was yesterday, maybe it’s right now. Well friend, I’ve got hopeful news for you.. YOU are not alone. Millions (and I’d argue billions) of people experience these same common pains in record scores. Never in history have we been so connected yet so emotionally and spiritually unknown. It’s as if it’s shameful or gross not to be happy. Well, I call bullshit on that. The fastest way to move back into joyful states is to tell someone else where you’re suffering. Sometimes just to feel seen again. To be known again. To be understood. To be accepted and loved on right where you are is all that you need to get back on your feet. You don’t need a solution or a seminar or a self-help book. Those things are good, but what you need can only be delivered by love and connection inside your community. It is the most powerful thing in the universe. And its exactly why this game exists today.
